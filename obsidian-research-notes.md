  OBSIDIAN RESEARCH NOTES TEMPLATE
  ==================================
  A rigorous research and study template for deep dives into any topic —
  academic research, business analysis, self-education, or curiosity-driven study.
  
  HOW TO USE:
  1. Copy to your Obsidian templates folder and trigger via Templater.
  2. Create one note per research topic or sub-topic (don't cram everything into one file).
  3. Fill in the Topic Overview section first to define your scope.
  4. As you read/watch/listen, add notes to Sources and Key Findings.
  5. Use the Questions section to track what you don't yet know — great for guiding further research.
  6. The Synthesis section is the most valuable: force yourself to write your own understanding
     from scratch, without looking at your notes.
  
  PRO TIP: Use Obsidian's backlinking to connect related research notes together.
  A "Map of Content" (MOC) note can link all notes on a broad topic.
