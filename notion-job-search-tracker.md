- ⬜ To Apply — on your radar, not yet applied

## 📬 Follow-Up Tracker

> Don't wait to be remembered. One well-timed follow-up can make the difference.

| Company | Context | Follow-Up Type | Sent Date | Response? |
|---------|---------|----------------|-----------|-----------|
| Acme Corp | Applied Jan 10 | "Just checking in" email | 2025-01-24 | No response |
| TechStartup | Post-interview | Thank you + summary | 2025-01-27 | ✅ Got reply |

> 💡 **Follow-up timing:** After applying → 1–2 weeks. After interview → same day or next morning. After no response in 2 weeks → one final check-in, then move on.
