**Status Key:**
- 💡 Idea — just a concept, not yet planned
- 📋 Planned — scheduled, brief written
- ✏️ Writing — actively being created
- 📝 Draft — first draft complete
- 🔍 Review — editing / feedback stage
- 📆 Scheduled — queued in scheduler
- 🎬 Filming / Recording — for video/audio content
- ✅ Published — live
- 🗃️ Archived — cancelled or saved for later
