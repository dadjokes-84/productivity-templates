  NOTION STARTUP DASHBOARD
  =========================
  An early-stage startup operating system for founders. Tracks metrics, OKRs, goals,
  investor updates, key decisions, and weekly priorities — all in one place.
  
  HOW TO USE:
  1. Paste this into a Notion page at the root of your startup workspace.
  2. Update Metrics Weekly (takes 5 minutes, saves hours of confusion).
  3. Set OKRs at the start of each quarter. Don't change them mid-quarter.
  4. Log every Key Decision when it's made — you'll forget the reasoning otherwise.
  5. Use the Investor Update section to draft your monthly/quarterly investor emails.
  6. The Weekly Sprint section replaces your weekly planning meeting if you're solo.
  
  PRO TIP: Share this page with your co-founder and investors (view-only link) so
  everyone is aligned without requiring a meeting.
